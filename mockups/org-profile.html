Paratext protects your data through internally managed backups in multiple secure locations. This automatically includes all your data in Paratext and no action is required.

Additional Service Continuity Backup: decide whether to opt in

In addition, an independent service continuity backup has been established as a safeguard for the Bible translation community by ETEN (Every Tribe Every Nation). It is maintained separately from Paratext's own infrastructure in a secure location, and would only be accessed by ETEN in the event of a catastrophic Paratext outage or other strict criteria agreed by the Paratext Board.

Paratext is committed to continuing our service to the Bible translation community in a sustainable fashion. This additional backup exists as a precaution. It is our firm intent that it will never need to be used.

What data is included? Translation files, revision history, notes, and project settings for all projects owned by this organisation.
How does this differ from Paratext's regular internal backups?
  • Paratext's internal backups are managed by the Paratext team. These include all Paratext data and no opt-in is required.
  • The service continuity backup is an independent separate copy held in a secure location. It would become accessible to ETEN's designated representative (Mission Mutual) only in the event of a catastrophic Paratext outage or other strict criteria agreed by the Paratext Board, so that ETEN could create an alternative service. This backup is opt-in.

SIL Pacific has enabled Service Continuity Backup for your organisation. An independent copy of your project data will be held in a secure location. It would become accessible to ETEN's designated representative (Mission Mutual) only in the event of a catastrophic Paratext outage or other strict criteria agreed by the Paratext Board, so that ETEN could create an alternative service. You can disable this at any time from your organisation's Service Continuity settings.

⚠ Reminder: Once enabled for a sub-organisation, only they can disable it.

⚠ Disable Service Continuity Backup?

Your organisation's data will no longer be included in the independent service continuity backup.

Sub-organisations that are currently backed up will not be affected — their backups will continue unless they independently choose to disable.

You can re-enable this at any time.
